# IncrementPilot White Paper

**A Governed Multi-Agent Platform for Turning Business Ideas into Verified Software Increments**

IncrementPilot is a governed software-delivery model that transforms broad business ideas into feasible, costed, designed, tested, demonstrated, deployable, and supportable product increments through specialized agents and explicit human approval gates.
